Dinh-Hieu Tran

HOME

Dinh-Hieu Tran (in Vietnamese: Trần Ðình Hiếu) was born and grew up in Gia Lai, Vietnam (1989). After high school (2007), he studied and earned his B.E. degree at Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering Department, Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology, Vietnam, in 2012. He already finished the Master degree in Electronics and Computer Engineering from Hongik University (Hons.), Korea, in 2017. From 2017 to 2018, he worked as a research assistant at Chungbuk National University. From 2018, he joined the Signal Processing and Satellite Communications (SIGCOM) group, Luxembourg University, headed by Prof. Björn Ottersten. He will be working on Proactive Caching for Future Wireless Networks, under the supervision of Prof. Symeon Chatzinotas. His major interests include Internet of Things , Wireless communications using unmanned aerial vehicles, B5G.
